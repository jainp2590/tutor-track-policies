Privacy Policy

App: Tutor Track · Developer: Abhishek Jain · Effective date: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tutor Track (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Tutor Track mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Who we are

Tutor Track is operated by Abhishek Jain. The app is listed on app stores as Tutor Track (Android package: com.abhishekjain05.tutortrack).

Contact: apptutortrack@gmail.com

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide and information generated by your use of the app.

Account and profile information

  • Email address and password (stored as a secure hash)
  • Role (teacher or student)
  • Profile details you choose to provide: full name, address, phone number, and gender
  • Email verification status

Coaching and educational content

  • Coaching center details (name, description, address, join codes)
  • Batch information, notes, announcements, and shared resources
  • Attendance records, fee invoices, and payment records
  • Student join requests, enrollments, doubts, test responses, and promotion posts
  • Files you upload (for example batch resources or promotion images), stored in secure cloud object storage

Device and usage information

  • App version, device platform (iOS or Android), and operating system version
  • In-app activity events (such as screen views and actions you take in the app)
  • Session identifiers used for analytics
  • Hashed IP address and user agent, when collected by our servers for analytics or security
  • Push notification tokens, if you allow notifications

Authentication and security data

  • Login sessions and refresh tokens
  • One-time passwords (OTPs) for password reset and email verification, stored temporarily in memory/cache and deleted after use or expiry
  • Technical error reports you or the app may send to help diagnose problems

3. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you
  • Provide core app features for teachers and students
  • Send transactional emails (such as password reset and email verification codes)
  • Deliver push notifications you have enabled
  • Improve reliability, security, and product quality
  • Understand how features are used through analytics
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

4. How information is shared

We may share information only in these situations:

  • Within the Service: Teachers and students see information appropriate to their role (for example, a teacher sees student attendance for their batch; a student sees resources shared with their batch).
  • Service providers: We use trusted infrastructure and service providers to host databases, cache, file storage, email delivery, push notifications, and error monitoring. These providers process data on our behalf under contractual safeguards.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the Service.

5. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal data associated with your account as described on our Account & Data Deletion page.

Some information may remain in encrypted backups for a limited period (typically up to 30 days) before being permanently removed. We may also retain certain records where required for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, access controls, and secure cloud storage for uploaded files. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your profile in the app
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as push notifications through your device settings
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Request a copy of personal data we hold about you

To exercise these rights, contact us at apptutortrack@gmail.com or follow the steps on our data deletion page.

8. Children’s privacy

Tutor Track is intended for use by teachers, coaching centers, and students in an educational context. The Service is not directed at children under 13 years of age to register on their own.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us without appropriate consent, please contact us at apptutortrack@gmail.com. We will take steps to delete such information.

Where students under 13 use the app, they should do so only with involvement and consent from a parent, guardian, or school/coaching center responsible for their account.

9. International users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer where permitted by applicable law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and change the effective date above. Continued use of the Service after changes become effective means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, email us at apptutortrack@gmail.com.
