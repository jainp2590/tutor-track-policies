Account & Data Deletion

App: Tutor Track · Developer: Abhishek Jain · Last updated: June 13, 2026

Request deletion of your Tutor Track account This page explains how to ask us to delete your account and the personal data linked to it. This URL is provided for the Google Play Store listing for Tutor Track by developer Abhishek Jain.

How to request account deletion

To delete your Tutor Track account, email us from the address registered on your account. We use this to confirm you own the account before deleting it.

  1. Open your email app and compose a new message to apptutortrack@gmail.com .
  2. Set the subject line to: Tutor Track Account Deletion Request.
  3. In the message body, include:
    • The email address registered on your Tutor Track account
    • Your role: teacher or student
    • A brief statement that you want your account and personal data deleted
  4. Send the email. We will reply to confirm receipt and may ask for additional verification if needed.
  5. After verification, we will delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days. We will email you when deletion is complete.

What we delete

When your deletion request is approved and processed, we delete personal data tied to your account, including:

Data type Deleted?
Account credentials (email, password hash) Yes
Profile information (name, address, phone, gender) Yes
Push notification tokens Yes
Login sessions and refresh tokens Yes
Analytics events linked to your user ID Yes
Student join requests, center enrollments, and batch memberships Yes
Teacher memberships at coaching centers you joined (not owned) Yes

What may be kept

Some data may be retained after account deletion for legitimate reasons:

  • Coaching center records you created as owner: If you are the sole owner of a coaching center, we may need to transfer ownership, close the center, or retain certain operational records required by other members (students and teachers). We will explain the outcome in our reply.
  • Historical educational and financial records: Attendance, fee invoices, payments, notes, or resources that form part of a coaching center’s records may be retained in anonymized or de-identified form where needed for the center’s legitimate business, tax, or audit purposes. Your direct identifiers (email, name, phone, address) are removed from those records where feasible.
  • Content uploaded by others: Materials uploaded by teachers or other users that do not identify you personally may remain available to authorized users of that center or batch.
  • Backups: Residual copies in encrypted backups may persist for up to 30 days, after which they are permanently removed through normal backup rotation.
  • Legal and security logs: Minimal records required to comply with law, resolve disputes, or prevent fraud may be kept for up to 90 days, then deleted or anonymized.
Before you delete your account Deletion is permanent. You will lose access to batches, resources, attendance history, and fee records associated with your account. Download or save any information you need before submitting your request.

Partial deletion without closing your account

If you only want to remove optional data without deleting your entire account:

  • Push notifications: Disable notifications in your device settings, then sign out of the app. Signing out removes your push token from our servers.
  • Profile updates: You can update your profile fields in the app where supported.
  • Other requests: Email apptutortrack@gmail.com and describe what you would like removed.

Processing time

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 7 business days and complete deletion within 30 days of verifying your identity. Complex cases (for example sole owner of an active coaching center) may take longer; we will keep you informed by email.

Questions

For privacy questions or help with deletion, contact apptutortrack@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
